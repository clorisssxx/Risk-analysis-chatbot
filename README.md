# Risk-analysis-chatbot

For the risk control department of a financial company, the types of risks involved can include credit risk, counterparty risk, market risk, and many others. Credit risk is primarily related to changes in the counterparty's creditworthiness, and the risk control department needs to monitor daily changes in credit utilization. Counterparty risk and market risk are mainly associated with margin calls. The risk control department must monitor the positions of counterparties daily, as well as price changes of the commodities they hold in the market, to determine whether margin calls are necessary. Additionally, there are many other risks specific to the company's business operations. A company may have numerous spreadsheets covering various aspects of its business. When someone unfamiliar with the company's operations, such as an intern, needs to find information or locate spreadsheets related to the company's business, they can only ask others or their mentors. Even for those who have been with the company for a long time, some business details can be easily forgotten.  

I have developed a conversational website where employees or this female employee can ask questions if they are unsure how to find information related to the company's business or the location of spreadsheets. They will first receive a brief guidance response, followed by a complete answer.
